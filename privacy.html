Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Royalist Clip Finder (the "Application") handles information. The Application is operated by Ezra Reichel (the "Operator", "we", "us"). Questions can be sent to ezra@getarrow.ai.

1. What the Application is

The Application is a private, first-party automation tool. It is used by the Operator to publish, schedule, and measure the performance of short-form video clips for The Royalist with Tom Sykes podcast on the Operator's own connected social media accounts (including TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram). It is not a consumer product, is not offered to the public, and does not create accounts for third-party end users.

2. Information we access

When the Operator authorizes the Application against a TikTok account, the Application uses the TikTok API solely in connection with that authorized account and accesses:

  • Basic account information (user.info.basic) — the connected account's basic profile details, used to confirm the authorized account.
  • The account's own video list and public statistics (video.list) — titles, descriptions, publish dates, and public counts such as views, likes, comments, and shares for videos on the connected account, used to measure content performance.
  • Video publishing (video.publish, video.upload) — to upload and publish video clips that the Operator has produced to the connected account.

The Application only accesses data belonging to the Operator's own authorized accounts. It does not collect, scrape, or store information about other TikTok users.

3. How we use information

  • To publish and schedule the Operator's video clips to the connected account.
  • To retrieve performance metrics for the Operator's own posts so the Operator can evaluate which content performs well.
  • To generate suggested captions and titles for the Operator's clips. Clip transcripts, titles, and related metadata may be processed by a third-party AI provider (Anthropic, the Claude API) for this purpose. No TikTok account credentials or third-party personal data are sent for this.

4. Storage and security

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored privately and used only to call the authorized platform APIs on the Operator's behalf. Tokens and collected metrics are stored either locally on the Operator's machine or in a private, access-controlled datastore. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties except the service providers strictly necessary to operate the Application (e.g. the platform APIs themselves and the AI caption provider described above).

5. Data retention

Tokens are retained until access is revoked or they expire. Collected performance metrics are retained for as long as they are useful for content analysis and may be deleted by the Operator at any time.

6. Revoking access

The Operator can revoke the Application's access at any time from the connected platform's settings (for TikTok: Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions). Revoking access stops all further API use and the stored tokens become invalid.

7. Children

The Application is an internal operational tool and is not directed to or intended for use by children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For any questions about this policy or the data the Application handles, contact ezra@getarrow.ai.
